### About Our Music

Our recordings capture the essence of live noisework and experimental sound. Each track is a journey through layered textures, processing chains, and sonic confrontation.

**Production & Recording:**
- Live recordings from performances and studio sessions
- Field recordings and found sound
- Analog and digital processing
- Raw, unpolished sound capturing the moment of creation
