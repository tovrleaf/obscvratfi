An experimental performance as part of the Tavern Brewery's underground music series. This session explores the sonic potential of industrial materials and feedback systems.

**Performance Details:**
- Start Time: 20:00
- Duration: 60 minutes
- Setting: Intimate underground venue
- Capacity: ~80 people

**What to Expect:**
Live synthesis and manipulation of harsh electronic and industrial textures. This is challenging, immersive sound art - not background music.

**Venue:**
Located in central Espoo with easy access via public transport. Limited parking available.
