A collaborative noisework session exploring the intersection of sound art and visual installation. In partnership with the Feedback Collective for an evening of experimental performance.

**Performance Details:**
- Start Time: 19:00
- Duration: 120 minutes (including intermission)
- Admission: Free (donations welcome)
- Gallery open for exhibitions during performance

**Collaboration:**
This event features Obscvrat alongside Feedback Collective in a joint improvisational session, responding to each other's sonic and visual contributions in real time.

**About the Space:**
Autonomous Gallery is a non-profit artist collective space dedicated to experimental and contemporary art. Intimate setting with experimental acoustics.
