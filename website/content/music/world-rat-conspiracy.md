<p>OBSCVRAT (Finland), feeds on crumbling textures, walks the death march of broken circuits, with infected field recordings and analog filth.</p>

<p>ROTAT (Finland) approaches with industrial violence and electroacoustic sickness. His unhinged tape manipulations and steel-scraping feedbacks vermin mutilation as audio ritual.</p>

<p>WHITERAT (Italy) destroys with blistering HNW and detuned power electronics. Whiterat tends to be calm, but can bite if it feel threatened.</p>

<p>Each project contributes an original ode to the rat, a symbol of filth, survival, rebellion, and gnawing truth. Strictly underground. Strictly unclean.</p>
